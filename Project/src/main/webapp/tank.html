Tank Bund Images

Tank Bund has a rich and fascinating history that dates back to the 16th century. It was built by the Qutb Shahi rulers as a dam to store water from the Musi River for irrigation and drinking purposes. The dam created a large artificial lake, which was named Hussain Sagar after Hussain Shah Wali, a sufi saint who helped in its construction.
