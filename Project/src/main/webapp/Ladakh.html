Ladakh Images

Introduction to Ladakh: Ladakh is a region located in the northern part of India, in the state of Jammu and Kashmir. It is known for its stunning landscapes, high mountain ranges, and unique cultural heritage. The region is often referred to as “Little Tibet” due to its strong influence of Tibetan Buddhism and culture.
