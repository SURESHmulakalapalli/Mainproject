Manali Images

Manali is a popular hill station located in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh. It is situated in the Beas River Valley and is surrounded by the majestic Himalayan mountains. Manali is known for its natural beauty, serene environment, and adventurous activities, making it a favorite destination for tourists from all over the world.
