Mysore Images

The Mysore Palace, also known as the Amba Vilas Palace, is a historical palace located in the city of Mysore in the southern state of Karnataka, India. It is one of the most famous tourist attractions in India and is renowned for its architectural beauty, intricate craftsmanship, and rich history.
