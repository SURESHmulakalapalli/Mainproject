Golconda Images

History of Golconda: Golconda, also known as Golkonda, is a historic fortress and ruined city located in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. The name Golconda is derived from the Telugu words “Golla Konda,” which means “Shepherd’s Hill.” The fort was originally constructed during the Kakatiya dynasty in the 13th century but was later expanded by the Qutb Shahi dynasty into a massive fortification complex
