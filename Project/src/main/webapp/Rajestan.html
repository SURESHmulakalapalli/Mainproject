Rajasthan Images

Introduction to Rajasthan: Rajasthan is a state located in the northwestern part of India. It is the largest state in terms of area, covering around 342,239 square kilometers. The state shares its borders with Punjab, Haryana, Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, and Gujarat. Rajasthan is known for its rich history, vibrant culture, majestic forts, and palaces.
