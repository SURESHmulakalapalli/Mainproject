Birla Mandir Images

History of Birla Mandir: The Birla Mandir in Hyderabad, also known as the Birla Temple, is a Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Venkateswara. It is located on a 280 feet high hillock called Naubath Pahad, which overlooks the Hussain Sagar Lake. The temple was constructed by the Birla Foundation in 1976 and is made entirely of white marble.
